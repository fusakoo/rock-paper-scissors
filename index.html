Let's Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Have your web browser's console open to view the gameplay.
To open console on your browser:

  • Firefox
    • From the menu: select "Browser Console" under the Web Developer submenu
    • From the keyboard: press Ctrl + Shift + J
    • Or: Press F12, and navigate to the Console tab
  • Google Chrome
    • From the menu: select "Developer Tools" under the More Tools submenu
    • From the keyboard: press Ctrl + Shift + J
    • Or: Press F12, and navigate to the Console tab
  • Other Browsers
